    Discover a new way of living

    Kelowna, British Columbia's third largest city, is home to a fast growing community. Parkside Residence is here to provide a clean living space that students can call home. Equipped with 22 student suites, Parkside Residence offers unparalled amenities and efficiency in accessing Kelowna's local community.
